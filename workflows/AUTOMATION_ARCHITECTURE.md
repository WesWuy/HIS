# HIS Automation Architecture

## Phase 1: Free Google Workspace Foundation

```text
Instagram content idea
↓
Content Pipeline row
↓
Script/caption generated manually with Claude/Codex
↓
Asset saved in Google Drive
↓
Status moved to Ready
↓
Wes manually posts to Instagram
↓
CTA sends people to Scorecard
↓
Google Form captures response
↓
Apps Script appends lead to Lead Tracker
↓
Gmail sends first email
↓
Wes reviews lead and follows up
```
