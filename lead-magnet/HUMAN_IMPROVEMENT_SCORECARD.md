# Human Improvement Scorecard

## Purpose

Capture leads from Instagram and give each person a clear first insight into their biggest improvement bottleneck.

## Suggested Google Form Questions

1. Name
2. Email
3. Instagram handle
4. What are you trying to improve most right now?
   - Energy
   - Discipline
   - Focus
   - Confidence
   - Health
   - Money
   - Purpose
   - Relationships
5. What is your biggest current obstacle?
6. How consistent are your daily habits? 1-10
7. How would you rate your energy? 1-10
8. How would you rate your focus? 1-10
9. How would you rate your stress? 1-10
10. Do you currently have accountability?
11. Are you interested in AI-supported coaching or systems?
12. What would change in your life if this improved in the next 90 days?
13. Are you open to paid support if the fit is right?

## Basic Lead Scoring Logic

- High pain + high urgency + open to paid support = high-ticket prospect
- High pain + medium urgency = mid-ticket prospect
- Curious but unclear = low-ticket or nurture
- No pain / no urgency = free nurture only
